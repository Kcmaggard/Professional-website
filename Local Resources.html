Library Resources in Lexington, KY

I am located in Lexington, Kentucky, the second largest city in Kentucky. Lexington has plenty of resources for local readers. In addition to the school libraries in all Fayette County schools, students and families have access to any of the six branches of the Lexington Public Libraries. The Central library is located in downtown Lexington and is the largest of the Library branches.

Visit the Lexington Public Library's website to find more information or to find the branch that is closest to you!
