Library Vision Statement

The vision of my school library is that the library is a space in which all members of the school community feel welcome. My vision is to create an inclusive library that empowers students and teachers to explore, connect, and grow, fostering a culture of inquiry and creativity. Students and faculty will develop a love of reading, as well as digital literacy skills for a successful future.

Library Mission Statement

The mission of my school library program is to promote lifelong reading for pleasure and information, to develop responsible consumers of technology, and to engage collaboration and creativity in all members of the school community.
